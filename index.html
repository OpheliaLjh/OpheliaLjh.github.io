About Me

I am a Senior student majoring in Computer Science at University of Michigan, with a dual degree of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Michigan - Shanghai Jiao Tong University Joint Institute.

My research interests lie in Automotive Security and Privacy as well as Deep Learning. I am currently working on anti-DoS attack mechanism for Controller Area Network (CAN) with Professor Kang G. Shin's PhD Mert Dieter Pesé. I am also a member of the Network, Communication & Information Systems Group under the supervision of Ranjan Pal and Mingyan Liu .
